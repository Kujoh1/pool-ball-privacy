Privacy Policy for PoolBall

Last updated: June 25, 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the PoolBall app ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Data Collection

PoolBall does NOT collect any personal data.

Our app:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not require registration or login
  • Does not collect email addresses or contact information
  • Does not track location data
  • Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

3. Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Game scores and high scores
  • Game progress and completed levels
  • Audio settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

PoolBall does not use any third-party services for analytics, advertising, or other purposes.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no sensitive information that needs to be protected. All local data is stored on your device and protected by your operating system's standard security measures.

6. Children

Our app is safe for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 or individuals of any age.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
