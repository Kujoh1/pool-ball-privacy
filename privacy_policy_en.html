Privacy Policy for PoolBall

Last updated: January 15, 2025

In short: PoolBall is completely free, ad-free, and collects no personal data whatsoever. Your privacy is important to us!

1. Overview

This privacy policy describes how the PoolBall app ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Data Collection

PoolBall collects NO personal data.

Our app:

  • Collects no personal information
  • Requires no registration or login
  • Collects no email addresses or contact information
  • Does not track location data
  • Uses no cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Contains no advertising or tracking networks
  • Sends no data to external servers

3. Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Game scores and highscores
  • Game progress and completed levels
  • Audio settings
  • Game preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

PoolBall uses no third-party services for analytics, advertising, or other purposes. The app is completely self-contained and ad-free.

5. Data Security

Since we collect no personal data, there is no sensitive information that could be transmitted or stored. All local game data is stored on your device and protected by your iOS system's standard security measures.

6. Children

Our app is safe for children of all ages. We knowingly collect no personal information from children or adults. The app is 100% ad-free and contains no external links or in-app purchases.

7. No Advertising or In-App Purchases

PoolBall is completely free and ad-free. There are no in-app purchases, no ad banners, and no hidden costs. Your gaming experience is never interrupted by advertising.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be published on this page. However, since we collect no data, the basic principles will not change.
